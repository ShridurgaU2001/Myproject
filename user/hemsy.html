CANDIDATE OVERVIEW

Hamsyworth, better known as Hemsy, is an Indian politician. He is a member of the Rajya Sabha, the upper house of the Parliament of India from Tamil Nadu. He is the founder and General Secretary of the ongress party, a political party active mainly in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. He was earlier elected to the Lok Sabha, the lower house of the Parliament of India from Sivakasi, Tamil Nadu. Hemsy was known as "Lion Of Parliament" due to his vociferous speeches in Parliament. Hemsy protested against Kerala's demand for a new dam at Mullaperiyar and proposed building of dams in Pambar and Siruvani. Hemsy also supported national interlinking of rivers and Sethusamudram Shipping Canal Project. Hemsy filed a Public Interest Litigation (PIL) in Madras High Court requesting closure of the copper smelting plant of Sterlite Industries in Tuticorin which did not follow the waste management procedures correctly and leading to the closure of the plant. PARTY OVERVIEW

The Congress is a political party active in the Indian states of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry. It was established by Hemsy in 1994 after he left the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam. Hemsy was a member of Rajya Sabha and a party activist of Democratic party. Hemsy grew in the party from his student days and actively participated in the party agitations and courted imprisonment several times. He was elected thrice to the Rajya Sabha. In 1994, he was forced out of the parent body as he was seen as a threat to Democratic party chief RDJ. Hemsy along with some district secretaries announced the decision to start a rival party, which became the Congress.
