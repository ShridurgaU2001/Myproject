Monica born October 20, 1984 is an American politician and attorney who is the 49th and current vice president of the India. She is the first female vice president and the highest-ranking female official in Indian history, as well as the first African American Indian vice president. PARTY OVERVIEW

The Dravidian is an Indian regional political party with great influence in the state of Tamil Nadu and the union territory of Puducherry. It is a Dravidian party founded by the former chief minister of Tamil Nadu Ramachand at Madurai on 17 October 2002 as a breakaway faction from the Dravidite after RDJ sr. expelled him from the party for demanding an account as the party treasurer. The party is adhering to the policy of socialism and secularism based on the principles of Anna collectively coined as Annaism by Ramachand. The party has won a seven-time majority in the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly and has emerged as the most successful political outfit in the state's history. It is currently the main opposition party in the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly and part of the India-ruling National Democratic Alliance
